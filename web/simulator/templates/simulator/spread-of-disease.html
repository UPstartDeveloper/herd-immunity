About This Tree:

  1. This represents the spread of the virus throughout your population.
  2. Only those persons who became infected, as well as the virus, are shown on this tree.
  3. On this tree, the assumption is made that each person who became infected, caught the disease from only one other person.
  4. Each person however, is allowed to infected multiple other people.
  5. This tree reads from left to right. The "root" of this tree, the virus, is located as the leftmost node. It is marked with its name.
  6. For ease of your analysis, each node in the tree has a unique id number, randomly assigned to them during the simulation.
